Frequently Asked Questions

PORTFOLIO MANAGER

Q1. How do I create a fund?

A1. A fund can be created in 4 simple steps.

1. Click on the "+ Create fund" icon to create a new fund. Then, choose an appropriate [Name] (no character limits) to attribute to the future fund.

2. Decide and select which assets you'd like to include in your fund by sliding "right" under the [Composition] column. Set a weight to each asset.

3. Assign a [Risk Rating] to the fund and add a [Description].

4. [Launch] the fund!

Q2. How many underlying assets can I select in a fund?

A2. Currently, in the first phase of the DEXTF mainnet release, a DEXTF fund can hold a maximum of 10 assets. We expect to increase the limit gradually and to eventually enable the creation of fund of funds.

Q3. What are the available assets that can be used in a fund?

A3. Currently, the available assets are:

  • Swappable on Kyber Network (see here for a full list):
    AMPL, BAT, BNT, COMP, DAI, KNC, LEND, LINK, LRC, MKR, MLN, QNT, REN, RSR, SNX, UNI, USDC, USDT, WBTC, WETH, YFI, ZRX

  • Currently non-swappable on Kyber Network:
    BAL, UMA
We will be releasing more and more assets in the next weeks (let us know if you have any preference). We expect to include all asset types, as tokenization efforts continue, including real world assets such as equity, debt, options etc. Stay tuned!

Q4. Can I create a fund with 1 underlying asset?

A4. No, the minimum is 2 assets. We expect to enable a fund of funds, which will still require a minimum of 2 funds.

Q5. What does the "symbol" represent in a fund?

A5. The symbol is a unique identifier that represents the fund on the platform's database. The limit is currently set at 3 alphanumeric characters

Q6. Does the risk rating influence any price or calculation in a fund?

A6. No, at the moment the risk rating is purely to inform the investor of the fund's overall risk rating based on assets' composition. However, we expect that it may be used for risk analytics purposes.

Q7. After creating a fund on the platform, it is not visible for an investor, why?

A7. After a fund is created on the platform, it needs to be launched on the blockchain to be visible by investors. Please make sure that you have completed this step.

Q9. How/Where can I define the management fee?

A8. At the moment the platform does not have management fees and this feature will be developed soon.

INVESTOR

Q1. Can I use a fiat currency (i.e. USD, EUR) to invest in a fund?

A1. No, our platform currently can only accept digital currencies.

Q2. Which (stablecoins) digital currencies can I currently use to invest in a fund?

A2. Our platform currently accepts DAI for new investments.

Q3. How many wallets does your platform support?

A3. At the moment we only support Metamask. We are working to integrate new wallets in the future.

Q4. I do not own any of the underlying assets in my wallet but I want to buy a share of the fund, what should I do?

A4. Our platform is integrated with Kyber Network (a decentralized exchange) which allows you to invest in a fund using DAI and buy the underlying assets on Kyber, through our platform, in a single transaction. Therefore, as long as you own sufficient DAI, you'll be able to complete the subscription into the fund.

Q5. I do not own all the underlying assets in my wallet but I have some and not enough to buy my target quantity, what should I do?

A5. Please refer to A4. In view of our Kyber integration you can swap the balance quantity required to subscribe to the fund without moving away from the platform.

Q6. Can I buy less than 1 token of a fund?

A6. Yes, XTF fund tokens are divisible in less than 1 unit.

Q7. Can I access the assets in the Safe at a later stage for future investments?

A7. Yes, this is indeed the purpose of the Safe, which is to allow investors to keep the funds available for future subscriptions/investment without withdrawing the assets back into the wallet.

Q8. Can I use a different exchange to purchase the underlying assets of a fund?

A8. No, at the moment our platform is integrated with Kyber but we are planning to add more exchanges in the future.

Q9. What is a market slippage when using Kyber Swap?

A9. The slippage is the difference between the price a transaction was executed and the price it was requested. On our platform you can decide to accept any market slippage or set a predefined amount in which case a transaction will be executed only if the slippage is less than your preset amount.

Q10. How do I calculate the required quantity of assets to invest in the fund?

A10. Given the XTF tokens quantity you'd like to buy in the [Instructions] tab, click on the revolving arrow icon next to [Required Quantity] column to obtain the required amount.
